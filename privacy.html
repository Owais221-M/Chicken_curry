Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Who We Are

Chicken Curry Messina ("we", "us", "our") is a restaurant based in Messina, Italy. We operate this website to allow customers to browse our menu and place orders online. This policy explains how we handle your personal data in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. What Data We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address (if applicable)
  • Order details and preferences
  • Payment method type (we do NOT store card numbers — payments are processed securely via Stripe)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To prepare and deliver your order
  • To send you an order confirmation email
  • To contact you if there are issues with your order
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

Your order data is retained for a minimum of 5 years to comply with Italian tax and accounting regulations, then securely deleted.

5. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to or restrict our processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@chickencurrymessina.com.

6. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc., a PCI-DSS compliant payment provider. We never store or transmit your full card details. You can read Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.
